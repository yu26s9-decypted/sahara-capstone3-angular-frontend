Conditions of Use

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Welcome to Sahara, the capstone marketplace where the desert competes with the rainforest and the legal page tries its best to look official. By using Sahara, you agree to use the site respectfully, responsibly, and with the understanding that this is a student project, not a billion-dollar legal department wearing a tiny suit.

Please Read These Conditions Carefully

These terms explain how you may use Sahara. If you browse products, create an account, sign in, add items to a cart, or stare at the navbar wondering if it is sticky enough, these conditions apply.

If any part of this page feels too serious, please remember: it was written for a capstone project. The code is real. The marketplace empire is still pending.

Your Account

You are responsible for keeping your login details safe. Please do not share your password, even if someone promises unlimited two-day shipping across the dunes.

If activity happens under your account, we may assume it was you, your keyboard, or a very determined autocomplete. Keep things secure.

Product Information

Sahara tries to show accurate product names, descriptions, prices, and images. Since this is a capstone project, some data may be sample data, placeholder data, or data that looked correct at 1:17 AM.

Product images belong to their respective owners or sources. Sahara uses them only to demonstrate a shopping experience, not to claim ownership of every headphone, hoodie, or suspiciously photogenic blender.

Orders, Carts, and Checkout

Adding an item to your cart does not guarantee inventory, delivery, happiness, or that you actually needed another gadget. Checkout features may be limited while Sahara is in capstone mode.

If something breaks during checkout, please be patient. Somewhere nearby, a developer is learning an important lesson about state management.

Privacy

We care about privacy. Sahara may store basic account/session data needed for login and app behavior. We do not want your secrets, your diary, or the emotional backstory behind your shopping cart.

For more details, review our Privacy Notice.

Electronic Communications

When you use Sahara, you may receive electronic messages from the app, such as confirmations, notices, or errors with surprisingly dramatic timing. You agree that digital communications count as written communications.

Acceptable Use

Do not misuse Sahara, attack the app, scrape it aggressively, upload harmful content, or attempt to make the cart hold negative twelve laptops. That last one is creative, but still no.

Disclaimer

Sahara is provided as-is for educational purposes. We make no promises that every feature is production-ready, lawyer-approved, or emotionally prepared for Black Friday traffic.
